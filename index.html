Alex Rodriguez currently sits in fourth place on the all-time home run list behind Barry Bonds, Hank Aaron and Babe Ruth. The Yankee designated hitter has a chance to pass Ruth before his career ends following the 2018 season, but will need to do some work to catch Aaron or Bonds.
